Hi, I'm Chloe.

I grew up in Sydney, Australia, and have also lived in Canberra, Melbourne, Germany, the Netherlands, the UAE, and occasionally, as a nomad.

I'm a born optimist, trained in economics and finance. My intellectual interests include entrepreneurship, innovation, and governance. I describe myself as a 'policy economist' to reflect my way of thinking.

It's been a pleasure to build a career, two businesses, and numerous community ventures, exploring the intersect of these themes.

I split my time across commercial projects, pro bono work, research studies, community-building and board duties; and in my downtime I rotate through various hobbies involving sport, art, and design.
