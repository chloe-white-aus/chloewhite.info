## Official Biography (Long)

Chloe White is one of Australia's most credentialled experts at the intersection of government, finance, and technology. Over a 15-year career in public policy and markets, her work has spanned key authorities including the federal treasury, the foreign service, industry associations, and a foundational advisory role with the Government of Dubai. As a virtual assets specialist since 2017, Chloe has shaped the rules that govern the industry across multiple jurisdictions.

Since founding Genesis Block Advisory in 2021 and riskmastery.xyz in 2023, Chloe has directly advised 45+ virtual asset projects across five continents, co-presented systemic risk research at the U.S. Federal Reserve, and chaired the Digital Assets Working Group at DECA, Australia's peak digital economy body; leading policy development for 70+ member organisations. She is recognised in the Top 50 Impact List three consecutive years (2023, 2024, 2025), a dual Blockies Award winner, and a recurring speaker at the world's most significant fintech stages.

## Official Biography (Short)

Chloe White is an independent advisor, board member, and former regulator specialising in new finance and technology. She has shaped regulation across multiple jurisdictions, from the Australian Treasury and the National Blockchain Roadmap, to Dubai's Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority. Chloe is appointed within a number of organisations including RMIT University in Melbourne and the BGIN think tank in Tokyo. Her clients have included international exchanges, asset managers, and listed companies, alongside industry associations.
